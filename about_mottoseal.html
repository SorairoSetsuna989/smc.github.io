- School Motto -

Ad Veritatem Per Caritatem - To Truth Through Charity

Definition:

It is in an atmosphere of trust and CHARITY that TRUTH can be sought and obtained. A relationship of sharing, openness, respect, and harmony among students, teachers and administrators is nurtured. Every person is recognized as equal in dignity.

The school aims to inspire students to love truth, knowledge and wisdom. The students are to abide in the virtue of charity by which they seek to grow in love for God and all that God loves.
